Step 1: Plastic Bottles

Used plastic bottles are collected from recycling programs, embarking on a journey toward a new life. These bottles serve as the base material for creating rPET fiber.

Step 2: Plastic Flakes

The bottles are meticulously cleaned, sorted, and chopped into small plastic flakes. These flakes become the building blocks for transforming waste into valuable fiber.

Step 3: rPET Yarn

The plastic flakes are melted down and spun into soft, durable yarn. This rPET yarn is strong and flexible and perfect for creating eco-conscious safety equipment.

Step 4: Durable Safety Gloves

Majestic uses rPET yarn to craft high-performance gloves. Each pair contributes to reducing plastic waste and supports a more sustainable future for workers and the planet.
